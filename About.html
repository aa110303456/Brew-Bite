Our Story

Brew & Bite is more than just a café; it's a place where moments are shared, routines are treasured, and friendships are made.

Brew & Bite, a tiny business project founded in 2009 in Central London by Emma Leo. This idea is driven by her passion for homemade cookies, freshly brewed coffee, and the natural joy of connecting people together. What began as a small neighborhood café swiftly transformed into a warm haven from the hectic city life, where discussions flowed as naturally as the coffee.

In Emma's opinion, great coffee should be combined with coziness, warmth, and authenticity. Every cup was specially brewed, every recipe was lovingly created, and every space was made to feel cozy.

Driven by the love and devotion of its customers, Brew & Bite now has over 3,000 locations in more than 30 countries; and is proudly serving millions of customers worldwide. The brand remains true to its core values despite its global growth.

Our Mission

Our goal at Brew & Bite is to create warm, inviting environments where people can relax, interact, and enjoy carefully brewed coffee along with freshly baked goods. We provide authenticity in all we do, along with comfort and quality in every cup. We work to make each visit feel comfortable, valuable, and memorable by fostering a sense of community, warmth, and reliability.
